The Gameplay

Every time you kill a player in PvP, you permanently steal one of their hearts and add it to your health bar. If an opponent kills you, you drop a heart block. Manage your health and gear up carefully.

Eliminations

If your health reaches zero hearts total, you are completely banned / eliminated from the map. Faction members or teammates must craft a revival beacon to bring your profile back into the active season layout.
